Fleet Week 2024 - Baltimore, MD

APA had the privilege of participating in one of the events for this year's Fleet Week at the harbor in Baltimore, MD. Fleet week is a longstanding Navy tradition that dates to 1935, when the first one was held in San Diego, CA. This event offers the opportunity for the public to have a chance to see some of the Navy's ships, learn their capabilities, and meet some of the most important people of the ship - the ship's crew.

This year, we were fortunate to tour the Littoral Combat Ship (LCS) 25, USS Marinette. It was a great opportunity to see how our support to the LCS shipbuilding program at a micro level contributes to the overall final product of a fully capable ship that measures roughly 387 ft, and is equipped with the finest sailors that are ready to perform any mission. It indeed was a great LCS Day!!

NSWC Indian Head Industry Day April 2024

Constellation-Class Guided Missile Frigate April 2024

This event provided an overview of NSWC IHD capabilities and insight into what solutions/services that is required by small businesses. National 8(a) Association National Small Business Conference February 2024

Sea Air Space Exposition April 2024

Attending the National 8(a) Small Business Conference is highly beneficial for your business, providing unparalleled networking opportunities with industry leaders, government officials, and potential partners. This premier event offers valuable workshops and seminars tailored to small businesses, covering crucial topics such as government contracting, business development, and financial management. By participating, you can gain insights into best practices, discover new business opportunities, and enhance your strategic planning. It's an ideal platform to showcase your capabilities, connect with key decision-makers, and drive your business growth to new heights.

APA was one of the roughly 2500 attendees for this event. It was a privilege to listen to Secretary of Navy, Honorable Carlos Del Toro at this event. As a former small business owner, he discussed the known struggles that small businesses have and has made a commitment to support the small business community. The Department of Navy (DON) office of Small Business Programs (OSBP) has been heavily engaged in their efforts to manage the Navy Program small business goals and to ensure there are plenty of opportunities made available for small businesses. Several big topics were discussed this year to include Artificial Intelligence (AI) and upcoming CMMC requirements. How is your business prepared? These are some “Big Rocks” for all of us small businesses.
