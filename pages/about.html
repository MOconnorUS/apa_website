About Us

Who We Are

APA is a minority-owned firm and a current participant in the Small Business Administration's (SBA) (8a) Business Development program. As a CVE Verified Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Business (SDVOSB) with a Secret Facility Clearance, a DCAA compliant accounting system, and CMMC Level II certification, we are committed to providing exceptional professional services support to government organizations.

Our Mission Statement

Achieve success by serving and supporting the client's mission efficiently and effectively.

Our Story

APA was established in 2013 to provide support to the US Military and create opportunities for veterans and their families. The founder, Terry D. Yarbrough, sought to offer meticulous support to challenge areas within the US Navy after retiring from 21 years of active-duty service.

"My primary goals are to provide accurate, relevant, and supportive information to assist with our employee and Client's needed tasks and/or inquiries, and to facilitate learning and productivity. I aim for APA to be a reliable and knowledgeable source across a wide range of offerings for guidance, solutions, and support tailored to the needs of APA's employees and its clients".


Leadership


APA was founded and led by a retired United States Naval Officer with extensive experience in DoD and CMS Acquisition, Finance, and Logistics. Along with the founder and with roughly 98% of our staff being veterans, we take pride in our robust workforce that executes in the clients best interest and operating under APA's core values:

People First Over Technology Philosophy

At APA, we prioritize the people first over technology philosophy to drive performance and achieve exceptional results. Our approach underscores the belief that while technology, including advancements in Artificial Intelligence (AI), is a valuable tool, the true power of any organization lies in its people. By focusing on comprehensive training, continuous professional development, and fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment we empower individuals to leverage technology, including AI, effectively.

This philosophy ensures that our team members are not only proficient in using technological tools but are also motivated, skilled, and adaptable, leading to enhanced productivity and innovation. Emphasizing people over technology is crucial because it builds a resilient and agile workforce capable of navigating and thriving through the complexities of modern organizational challenges.

Recognizing the transformative potential of AI, we are committed to gradually learning and integrating this new technology alongside the government's evolving approach. Our organization stays informed about the latest AI advancements and government policies, ensuring that we align with regulatory requirements and best practices. We engage in ongoing AI training programs and maintain an open dialogue with government agencies to responsibly and ethically implement AI solutions.

By placing people at the core of our strategy and progressively learning AI technology, we ensure that our workforce is well-equipped to harness the benefits of AI. This measured approach leads to sustainable growth, improved job satisfaction, and better overall organizational performance. Our commitment to continuous learning and adaptation ensures that our people drive the success of AI and other technology implementations, ultimately leading to a more innovative and competitive organization.
